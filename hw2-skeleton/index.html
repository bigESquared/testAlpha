ANTHRO 169 Field Practicum

The goal was to observe & analyze a given locus, in this case Davis Park located in a co-op in southside Berkeley, as an interactive space that encourages social gathering through its design & layout. Observation of bodily orientations, proxemics, language & metalangue use revealed insight on membership & how the space is bounded for interactions.
